# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we expect
participants to behave professionally and respectfully.

## Expected Behavior

- Be considerate and collaborative.
- Help each other and provide constructive feedback.
- Avoid harassment, abuse, hate speech, and discrimination.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Trolling or insulting comments.
- Public or private harassment.
- Discrimination of any kind (race, gender, religion, disability, etc.).

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the project
maintainer via issue or email. Reports will be treated confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, issue warnings, or block users to maintain
a safe environment.
